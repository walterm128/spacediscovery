INTRODUCTION

Our innovative idea is to offer the rental or sale of a modular Satellite Kit, and the service of assembly in orbit of the same one, by means of the station of assembly designed for such aim. The customer will send the instrument to be installed with the necessary connections to be able to integrate it into the kit with the specifically designed modules.

Features

MODULAR

Modular satellites have the advantage, over monolithic satellites, that can acquire even larger sizes and add functionalities that do not yet exist in the satellite. They can even be upgraded in orbit.

Customizable

The satellite kit will have slots where the client module(s) will be attached. Being able to be flexible in its design as long as it maintains the standard with the rest of the Kit.

Low Cost

In the case of the launch, the saving decreases even further, as the customer only has to send the instrument. Not only can it be sent in smaller launchers, due to the lower volume compared to a complete satellite, but also shipments can be shared using the payload capacity between several customers.

Satellite Rental

While this already considerably reduced the costs to access space, we wanted to go for more, so we also offer the opportunity to rent a satellite already armed for a minimum period to be defined.
